Delivery Information

We are working to ensure Standard Delivery within 3-5 days and we're now offering Next Day Delivery.

When will my order arrive?

If you have selected a downloadable product this will be sent to you immediately after payment has been confirmed. However due to the nature of the internet than can sometimes be a delay of the message leaving our servers and arriving at you mail provider. For postage orders we use FedEx, Hermes, Royal Mail, Parcelforce or XDP to deliver all of our orders. If you have provided us with a mobile number then the Courier may send you an SMS (text) message on the day that your delivery is due. A shipping confirmation email will advise on which courier service has been allocated. If you have selected Next Day, please make sure that you are at home to receive your order as a signature as proof of delivery is required. Some postcodes in Scotland take longer for us to reach and we will let you know if this is the case. Unfortunately, UK deliveries that involve a journey across water can take up to two extra days to arrive.

Delayed orders

Some carriers and orders will need to be signed for, and if no one is available to accept delivery at the specified delivery address, the package will be returned to your local delivery depot and a card will be left detailing how to arrange redelivery of your order. Look out for email and text messages from the courier.

PO Box and BFPO Deliveries

Unfortunately, we are unable to process orders for PO Box and BFPO addresses.

Sustainable Packaging

Alongside our Eco product ranges, our brown cardboard shipping boxes are 100% Carbon Neutral*. With over 80%** of our orders utilising our 100% Carbon Neutral packaging, we are helping to reduce our carbon footprint.

* This statement is true to shipments fulfilled by INVITATIONS, but cannot guarantee the sustainability of Third Party Packaging

** This applies to shipments fulfilled directly by INVITATIONS
